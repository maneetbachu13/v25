Revanth

A powerful vocalist from India, L.V. Revanth has enthralled music fans all throughout the country with his soul-stirring performances and dynamic voice. Well-known for his remarkable versatility and variety He became well-known across the country after winning Season 9 of Indian Idol, including famous judges and musical icons. After this victory, his career took off. Revanth has played live all throughout India and overseas, sharing stages with legendary performers and captivating audiences with his emotional connection and high-energy performances.

DJ NIKITA

​Nikitha Ejanthkar, professionally known as DJ NIKITA, is an Indian DJ and music producer who has made significant strides in the international music scene. Transitioning from acting to music, she has performed over 200 shows worldwide. Her versatility spans various genres, and her performances are known for creating unforgettable experiences. In 2024, she achieved the notable rank of #67 in Asia's DJ rankings. Beyond her live performances, DJ NIKITA has a strong online presence, sharing her music and engaging with fans through platforms like Instagram, Youtube.

AFTERALL

​Vaibhav Gaur, professionally known as AFTERAll, is a Mumbai-based DJ and music producer recognized for his dynamic presence in the Indian dance music scene. With over five years of experience, he has cultivated a diverse musical repertoire encompassing genres such as hip-hop, house, Bollywood, commercial, and Punjabi music. Throughout his career, AFTERAll has performed at numerous music festivals and clubs across North India. His talent has garnered attention from prominent platforms like VH1, Rolling Stone, and New Indie India, and he has been featured on Spotify editorials.
