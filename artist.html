Revanth

A powerful vocalist from India, L.V. Revanth has enthralled music fans all throughout the country with his soul-stirring performances and dynamic voice. Well-known for his remarkable versatility and variety He became well-known across the country after winning Season 9 of Indian Idol, where his stirring performances captured the hearts of millions of people, including famous judges and musical icons. After this victory, his career took off, and he went on to provide the voice of many popular movie songs, garnering both critical and popular acclaim. Revanth has played live all throughout India and overseas, sharing stages with legendary performers and captivating audiences with his emotional connection and high-energy performances.
